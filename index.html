The Regulatory Paradox

Enterprises today face a fundamental conflict between two critical legal mandates. QRVEF is the bridge that resolves this mathematical and legal deadlock.

GDPR Article 17

The "Right to Erasure" requires organizations to completely remove personal data upon request, including from backups and logs.

VS

MiFID II / AI Act

Financial and AI regulations mandate immutable, tamper-proof logs for auditability, preventing any modification or deletion of records.

Verifiable Crypto-Shredding

Instead of attempting to delete scattered data, QRVEF encrypts every record and surgically destroys the specific Data Encryption Key (DEK).
